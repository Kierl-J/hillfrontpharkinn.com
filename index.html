About Us

A Retreat Like No Other

Nestled in the scenic hills of Dumanjug, Cebu, Hillfront Phark Inn offers an unparalleled escape from the everyday. Our hotel combines comfortable accommodations with warm Filipino hospitality to create memories that last a lifetime.

Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, a family adventure, or a peaceful retreat, our dedicated staff ensures every moment of your stay exceeds expectations. Discover the beauty of Central Visayas from our doorstep.
